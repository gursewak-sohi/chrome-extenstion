G-SHOCK Disrupts the Matrix With Digital Glitch Series
  • G-SHOCK drops a three-piece capsule, unveiling a new graphic pattern inspired by digital noise and static.
  • The Digital Glitch series includes models GA-2100NN-1A, GA-2200NN-1A and DW-5600NN-1. The watches feature a semitransparent band punctuated by a polarized ink print that evokes the image of an electric current glitching mid-transmit.
  • Using the latest practices in print technology, G-SHOCK’s futuristic series channels a stylish, fashion-forward offering for watch enthusiasts.
  • Signature to the brand, each timepiece provides 200-meter water resistance and shock resistance. All three models are equipped with flash alert, a double LED light or EL back light, a multi-function alarm, a 1/100-second stopwatch and a countdown timer.
  • For the distorted static effect, G-SHOCK’s multi-angle printing deposits microparticles that reflect light at varying angles.
